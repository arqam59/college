About Us

About
Darul Arqam

وَقُل رَّبِّ زِدْنِي عِلْمًا

"And say: My Lord, increase me in knowledge." — Quran 20:114

Affiliated with Jamia Nooria Arabiyya Pattikkad, Darul Arqam offers a unique blend of religious and contemporary education. Located in the heart of Perinthalmanna, Malappuram, we provide an environment conducive to spiritual growth and intellectual rigor.

Our institution stands as a beacon of Islamic scholarship in Kerala, combining the depth of classical Shareea'h sciences with the breadth of modern arts and humanities.
